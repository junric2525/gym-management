Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is the Charles Gym Management System?

It’s an online platform designed to help manage gym operations such as membership registration, attendance tracking, payment monitoring, and staff management all in one place.

2. How do I create a new User account?

You can register through the system’s sign-up page. Fill in the required details like your name, email, then submit the form.

3. I forgot my password. What should I do?

Click the “Forgot Password” link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and you’ll receive instructions on how to reset your password.

4. Can I update my personal information?

Yes. After logging in, go to your Profile page and click “Edit Information.” Make your changes and save them to update your account details.

5. How can I renew my membership?

You can renew your membership through the Membership section. Choose your desired plan, make a payment, and your new validity period will update automatically.

6. Are my payments secure?

Yes, all payment information is encrypted and securely processed through trusted payment gateways. Charles Gym does not store sensitive financial details.

7. Can staff members access my personal information?

Only authorized staff members with the appropriate access level can view or update your account. All staff are bound by confidentiality agreements.

8. What happens if I miss my gym payments?

Your account may be temporarily inactive until payment is settled. Once payment is confirmed, your account will automatically regain access.

9. Can I cancel my membership?

Yes, members can request cancellation through the system or by contacting the front desk.

10. How can I provide feedback about the system?

You can submit feedback or report issues through the “Contact Us” or “Support” page. Your input helps us improve the system’s features and usability.
